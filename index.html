Intresting Facts About Camels

  1. There are two types of camels: One humped or “dromedary” camels and two humped Bactrian camels
  2. Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  3. Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat.

Intresting Facts About Chameleon

  • Chameleons are reptiles which are the part of the iguana suborder
  • Chameleon's skin color is an important part of communication among them
  • Most Chameleons have prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree baranches

Intresting Facts About Cicada

  1. Cicadas can survive a huge fall as babies, or nymphs
  2. The loud whirring or buzzing sound you hear is an all-male cicada chorus
  3. Most cicadas have red-orange eyes

Intresting Facts About Panda

  • Pandas go from pink to white and black (or brown).
  • Pandas have so many fans because they look cute.
  • An adult panda can eat 12–38 kilos of bamboo per day!

Intresting Facts About Pigeon

  1. Pigeons are extremely complex and intelligent animals
  2. Pigeons are known for thier outstanding navigational skills
  3. Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities

Intresting Facts About Teddy Bear

  1. The term bear-hug was first recorded in 1846.
  2. The Oxford English Dictionary dates the first use of the term teddy bear to 1906
  3. In 1902 in Germany, Steiff launched “Bear 55 PB”, the first toy bear with jointed arms and legs.
